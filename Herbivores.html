Herbivores

Here is the table that I put together that lists all available Herbivores for the Canada Challenge mode.

The key to achieving that illusive five star rating seems to be maximizing Species per enclosure and popularity of the dinosaurs. So that means having multiple different kinds of hebivores in an enclosure that all have the same dietary needs and are a liked species or companion makes the dinosaurs happier and the park guests more enticed.
